# Retail Financial Performance Overview

## Project Description
This project focuses on analyzing and visualizing **retail financial performance** using **SQL and Power BI**.  
The objective is to provide a clear overview of sales, cost, profit, customers, and product performance through an interactive dashboard.

The project emphasizes **data modeling, business KPIs, and dashboard storytelling**, not predictive analytics or automation.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** – Database schema design and table creation
- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and visualization

---

## Database Design (SQL)
The database was designed using structured tables to represent a retail sales environment.

### Key Tables
- **Customers** – Customer-level details
- **Products** – Product name and category information
- **Sales / Transactions** – Sales quantity, revenue, and cost
- **Date / Time reference** – Used for monthly analysis

The tables were created using SQL and later connected to Power BI for reporting and analysis.

---

## Power BI Dashboard Overview

### 1. Executive Overview
Displays high-level KPIs to quickly assess financial health:
- **Total Cost**
- **Total Sales**
- **Total Profit**
- **Profit Margin (%)**

These KPIs are calculated using DAX measures.

---

### 2. Monthly Performance Analysis
- **Monthly Net Profit**  
  Bar chart showing profit distribution across different months.
  
- **Monthly Revenue vs Cost Trend**  
  Line chart comparing revenue and cost trends over time.

This section helps identify monthly performance fluctuations.

---

### 3. Customer Insights
- **Top Customers by Revenue**  
  Visual ranking customers based on total sales contribution.

- **Customer-wise Sales and Profit Table**  
  Displays total sales and total profit for each customer.

Useful for identifying high-value and low-performing customers.

---

### 4. Product Profitability
- **Sales vs Profit by Category**  
  Comparison of sales and profit across categories such as:
  - Electronics
  - Furniture
  - Accessories

- **Product-level Performance Table**  
  Includes:
  - Total Profit
  - Total Quantity Sold
  - Total Sales per product

Helps evaluate which products and categories drive profitability.

---

### 5. Filters & Interactivity
- State-wise filters:
  - Delhi
  - Karnataka
  - Maharashtra
  - Tamil Nadu
  - Telangana
- Category-based filtering
- Fully interactive visuals for dynamic analysis

---

## DAX & Calculations
Key metrics such as:
- Total Sales
- Total Cost
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin

were calculated using **DAX measures** within Power BI.
